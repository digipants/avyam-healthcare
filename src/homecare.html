FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Why Choose Avyam for Homecare Services?

Avyam offers expert home healthcare services with trained professionals, personalized care plans, and 24/7 support, ensuring your loved ones receive hospital-quality care in the comfort of home.

How Can I Book a Home Visit?

You can book a home visit by calling our support team or using our online booking form. We ensure quick confirmations and timely visits by our trained healthcare professionals.

What Types of Homecare Services Do You Provide?

Avyam provides nursing care, physiotherapy, elderly care, lab sample collection, medical equipment rental, and post-surgical support—all at your doorstep.

Are Your Nurses and Caregivers Certified?

Yes, all our nurses and caregivers are certified, experienced, and undergo regular training to maintain the highest standards of home healthcare delivery.

Can I Get Lab Tests Done at Home?

Yes, we offer lab sample collection at home with fast reporting. Our phlebotomists ensure safe and hygienic sample collection.

Do You Offer Full-Body Check-Up Packages?

Yes, Avyam provides comprehensive full-body check-up packages at home, including sample collection and doorstep report delivery.

Do You Provide Vaccination at Home?

Yes, we offer home vaccination services for flu, COVID-19, hepatitis, and more—administered by qualified professionals.

How Quickly Can I Get Services After Booking?

We aim to provide services within 2 hours of booking in major cities. Our team ensures timely coordination for all appointments.
